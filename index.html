About

Passionate about game design and development, I specialize in UE5.
With over two years of experience in game development, I have honed my skills in C++, Blueprints, and Gameplay Ability System (GAS) to design and implement complex game mechanics, systems, and interactive features.
My expertise bridges the gap between design and engineering, allowing me to prototype, iterate, and refine gameplay with a strong technical foundation. I thrive on solving technical design challenges, optimizing gameplay systems, and ensuring seamless player experiences.
My goal is to continue learning and growing as a Technical Game Designer, contributing to the creation of innovative and captivating games.

Mars Marine

"Mars Marine" is a thrilling mini game developed as part of the "Unreal Engine Blueprint Game Developer Online Course". It immerses players in a high-stakes scenario on a fantasy Mars, where they must fend off increasingly formidable waves of alien invaders to protect valuable ancient ruins harboring a priceless treasure. Players must strategically engage and defeat the alien threats while also utilizing healing pick-ups scattered throughout the map to survive.
