Mars Marine

Description

"Mars Marine" is a thrilling mini game developed as part of the "Unreal Engine Blueprint Game Developer Online Course". It immerses players in a high-stakes scenario on a fantasy Mars, where they must fend off increasingly formidable waves of alien invaders to protect valuable ancient ruins harboring a priceless treasure. Players must strategically engage and defeat the alien threats while also utilizing healing pick-ups scattered throughout the map to survive.

Key Features

  • Intense Alien Wave Defense
  • Tactical Shooting Mechanics
  • Ruins Protection Objective
  • Dynamic Healing Mechanism
  • Immersive Martian Environment
  • Blueprint Scripting for Gameplay Logic
  • Immersive Audio Design
