Obstacle Assault

Description

In 'Obstacle Assault', a 3D lowpoly pirate-themed platformer, players assume the role of a courageous pirate tasked with retrieving elusive treasure maps concealed within bottles, scattered across perilous terrains. Navigating through treacherous obstacles, including bubbling lava and deep waters, demands precision and agility. With each map collected, players inch closer to the captain.
